Wynn Type C & D Mk5 – Heavy Duty Straight Line Wipers

The heavy duty Type D (externally mounted motor) and Type C (internally mounted motor) straight line wipers offer the most advanced design of linear action window wiping systems for marine and other specialty applications.

A large wiped window coverage can be achieved, providing maximum view from the ship's bridge whatever the weather, in temperatures from as low as -30ºC to extreme tropical conditions.

Key Features

  • Heavy duty construction for demanding marine environments
  • Choice of internally (Type C) or externally (Type D) mounted motor
  • Excellent wiped area for maximum bridge visibility
  • Reliable performance from -30°C to extreme heat
  • Compatible with Wynn wash systems and controllers
