Wynn Wiper Controllers

Advanced control solutions for all Wynn wiper systems. From simple intermittent to complex synchronized multi-wiper installations with heater and wash integration.

800 Controllers

Offered with and without power supply unit (PSU). Easily controlled in both low and high speed forms. Variable intermittent and size compatible with standard switches.

800 Control 24v (PDF) 800 Control w/PSU (PDF)
1000 Controllers

Fast continuous speed mode and intermittent mode with 4 pre-set delay times (4-20 seconds). Automatic self parking and auto-shut off safety feature. Ideal for all straight line wiper systems and compact for easy installation.

2000 Controllers

Individual and group control with fast & slow speed operations. Intermittent mode with 15 delay positions and automatic self parking.

Series 2000 brochure (PDF)
3000 Controllers

Individual or group control with fast & slow speed operations. Intermittent mode with 3 delay timings. Group heater switch and small size control panel.

Series 3000 brochure (PDF)
6000 Controllers

Fulfills requirements for all small and complex systems. Can be locally controlled or from master control system with variations in voltage control and synchronisation of wipers in low & high speeds.

8000 Controllers

Individual and group control features with multi-speed operation. Variable intermittent functions with programmable timing and synchronized control during operation. Self-parking feature and separate control of wiper de-icing heaters, heated windows, and wash/wipe/air purge function.
